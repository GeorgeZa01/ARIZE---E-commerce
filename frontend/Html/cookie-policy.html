Cookie Policy

Welcome to our Cookie Policy page. Your privacy is important to us, and we want to be transparent about how we use cookies on our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us improve your browsing experience by remembering your preferences, enhancing security, and providing analytical insights. Some cookies are temporary and expire after your session, while others persist to recognize you on future visits.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function properly. These include authentication and security cookies.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyze website traffic and improve functionality by collecting anonymous data.
  • Functional Cookies: Remember user preferences such as language and theme settings to personalize your experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track the effectiveness of marketing campaigns.
  • Third-Party Cookies: Set by external services such as social media platforms or analytics providers.

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions correctly
  • Enhance user experience by remembering settings and preferences
  • Analyze website traffic, trends, and performance metrics
  • Deliver personalized content and advertisements tailored to user interests
  • Improve security by detecting and preventing fraudulent activities

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are placed by third-party services, including analytics providers (e.g., Google Analytics), social media platforms, and advertising networks. These cookies allow third parties to track your browsing activity across different websites.

How to Manage Your Cookies

You have full control over your cookie preferences. You can:

  • Adjust browser settings to block or delete cookies
  • Opt out of marketing cookies through ad settings
  • Use privacy-focused browser extensions to manage tracking
  • Visit www.aboutcookies.org for more information on cookie management

Note: Disabling certain cookies may affect website performance and functionality.

Your Consent

By continuing to use our website, you agree to our use of cookies as outlined in this policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by adjusting your cookie preferences.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy periodically to reflect changes in technology, regulations, or our services. Please review this page regularly for updates.
